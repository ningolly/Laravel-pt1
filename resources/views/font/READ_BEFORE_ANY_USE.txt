Atemica Sans is a sturdy and versatile sans-serif, designed with both readability and aesthetic in mind. Its letterforms are constructed from simple geometric shapes, resulting in a contemporary, almost technical look. The even distribution of weight and open counters make it a strong choice for any project that requires clarity and a clean look. Use it for branding, signage or body text.

This font has been created with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring a smooth and consistent appearance across all glyphs.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Mans Greback has crafted this typeface with a focus on a geometric structure and high readability. It's a perfect blend of modern and timeless design.
